My Favorites

1. Favorite Food

Anything that my mom cooks. Ice cream because it gives me comfort when I'm sad. When it comes to fruits, I want mango, pineapple, rambutan, banana, and apple.

2. Favorite Hobbies

Everyday I enjoy scrolling on social media and reading the latest chismis or news about what's happening in the Philippines. I also like reading articles about interesting facts, news, artist lifestyles, and many more. In my free time, I enjoy cooking. I learned to cook when I was young. I love to sleep, listen to music, and watch movies—my favorite genres are romance and comedy. Sometimes I love playing games like Roblox and other simulator games. I enjoy solving jigsaw puzzles and word search games as my stress relievers. I always love to talk with my family and friends, whether in person or through calls. I’m the type of person who is a good listener and I also love journaling.

3. Favorite Music / Artists

My favorite music genres are pop, K-pop, and OPM. I often listen to artists like Avril Lavigne, Britney Spears, Dua Lipa, Ariana Grande, Billie Eilish, Olivia Rodrigo, Adele, Sabrina Carpenter, Camila Cabello, SZA, Beabadoobee, Katy Perry, Keshi, Twice, New Jeans, Doja Cat, Taylor Swift, Bruno Mars, Shawn Mendes, Harry Styles, and Justin Bieber. For local OPM, I love 6cyclemind, Cueshé, Cinderella, Ogie Alcasid, Regine Velasquez, Jolina Magdangal, MAKI, Early Agustin, December Avenue, IV of Spades, Nina, Kyla, Yeng Constantino, TOONEJAY, MYMP, Rey Valera, April Boy, Gloc-9, and Abra.

One of my favorite songs is "Bata, Dahan-Dahan" by IV of Spades because it talks about the kids nowadays. IV of Spades is one of my all-time favorite OPM bands. Some of their songs are deep and meaningful. This band helped me during the COVID pandemic; their songs healed me. I also love Gloc-9 and Abra because their songs talk about society and contain life lessons. I really like songs with deep meanings because they inspire me.

4. Favorite Movies / Series

I enjoy watching Disney movies and 90’s Filipino romance films. My favorite Disney movies are Tangled, Mulan, and Frozen. I also love 90’s romance films like:

  • Claudine Barreto and Rico Yan movies such as Dahil Mahal na Mahal Kita and Got 2 Believe
  • Jolina Magdangal and Marvin Agustin movies like Labs Kita, Okay Lang?

These movies inspire me and also help me relax.

5. Favorite Places

One of my favorite places to visit is the mall because it makes me feel calm and refreshed. I also love visiting my aunt’s house in Calamba, Laguna. The people and the surroundings there heal me and make me feel at peace.
